US Rental Investment Markets

Where should you buy rental property in the US? This page ranks 3797 cities by investment metrics derived from Zillow’s public home value and rent indices. The price-to-rent ratio is the standard benchmark:

  • Strong Buy (P/R < 15): Cash flow positive from day one
  • Moderate (P/R 15–20): Reasonable returns, some appreciation upside
  • Lean Rent (P/R 20–25): Marginal; depends on appreciation
  • Rent Favored (P/R > 25): Buy only if you’re betting on price growth

Data Sources

All data is traceable and reproducible. Run Rscript scripts/us_rental_markets_data.R to refresh.
